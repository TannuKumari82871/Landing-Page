VARIETIES

Alphonso Mango

Known for its rich flavor and aroma, Alphonso mangoes are a favorite worldwide. They ripen from late April to early June.

Kesar Mango

Kesar mangoes are known for their bright orange pulp and sweet taste. They are available from May to July.

Dusheri Mango

Originating from Northern India, Dusheri mangoes are sweet and tangy, perfect for making chutneys and desserts. They ripen from mid-June to late July.

Himsagar Mango

Himsagar mangoes are juicy with a fiberless pulp, making them ideal for fresh consumption. They are available from May to June.

Langra Mango

Langra mangoes have a unique taste and are known for their green skin even when ripe. They are harvested in late June.

Totapuri Mango

Totapuri mangoes are less sweet and often used in pickles and salads. They are available from May to July.
